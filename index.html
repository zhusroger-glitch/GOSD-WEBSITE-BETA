About GOSD 关于GOSD

GOSD (Global Outdoor Sports & Tourism for Sustainable Destination) is a platform dedicated to connecting leading outdoor sport and tourism service providers from around the world and China to foster positive cooperation, drive mutual growth, and promote innovative solutions and models that enable healthy and impactful destinations. GOSD（全球户外运动与可持续旅游目的地平台）致力于连接全球和中国的领先户外运动与旅游服务提供商，促进积极合作，推动共同成长，推广创新解决方案和模式，打造健康且有影响力的旅游目的地。

Who are we 我们是谁

As the organizer of GOSD, SHOWTRUE China (绣球科技), founded in 2014, has extensive experience in organizing sports and tourism events. It is one of the pioneering companies in customized outbound tourism and thematic destination marketing in China. We are recognized as an innovative player in the Chinese market and were among the first to successfully introduce and promote international destinations, significantly boosting their brand visibility and commercial performance. 作为GOSD的组织者，绣球科技（SHOWTRUE China）成立于2014年，在体育赛事和旅游活动组织方面拥有丰富经验。公司是中国定制出境旅游和主题目的地营销领域的先驱企业之一。我们被视为中国市场的创新者，是最早成功引进和推广国际目的地、显著提升其品牌知名度和商业表现的公司之一。
