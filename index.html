About GOSD 关于GOSD

GOSD (Global Outdoor Sports & Tourism for Sustainable Destination) is a platform dedicated to connecting leading outdoor sport and tourism service providers from around the world and China to foster positive cooperation, drive long-term growth, and promote innovative solutions and models that enable healthy and impactful destinations. GOSD（全球户外运动与旅游可持续目的地）是致力于连接全球和中国的领先户外运动与旅游服务提供商的平台，促进积极合作，推动长期成长，推广创新解决方案和模式，打造健康且有影响力的旅游目的地。

To advance strategic projects and leverage collective expertise, we are establishing the GOSD Alliance. Through tailored initiatives and a structured membership program, the Alliance will grant members priority access to capacity-building, visibility, and partnership opportunities.
